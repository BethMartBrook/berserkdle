Guess a Berserk Character

Enter the name of any character from the Berserk universe in the search box. The game will show you how close your guess is to the secret character.

Understand the Feedback

After each guess, you'll see how each attribute compares to the secret character:

Correct: Attribute matches exactly
Incorrect: Attribute does not match
3

Refine Your Next Guess

Based on the feedback, adjust your next guess to get closer to the secret character.

üí° Strategy Tip:

If you see multiple green attributes, try characters that share those traits. If you see red attributes, eliminate characters with those traits.

Attribute Guide

Each character has these attributes that you'll need to match:

Name
The character's full name or most common name
Gender
Male or Female
Hair Colour
The character's primary hair color
Weapon
Their primary weapon or fighting style
Affiliation
Current or most notable group/organization
Species
Human, Apostle, Elf, etc.
